Welcome to SmartScan

SmartScan is a professional QR code and barcode scanning application designed for users who value efficiency and privacy.

Core Features

  • Fast Scanning - Supports QR codes, barcodes, and more
  • Batch Scanning - Continuously scan multiple QR codes with smart deduplication
  • QR Code Generation - Generate various types of QR codes
  • Scan History - Auto-save records for easy access
  • Multi-language Support - Supports Chinese and English

Privacy Protection

SmartScan takes your privacy seriously:

  • All data stored locally
  • No information uploaded to servers
  • No personal information collected
  • Works completely offline
